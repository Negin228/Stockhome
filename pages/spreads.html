Mean Reversion Spread Candidates

Call Debit Spread (Bullish): Buy a call at a lower strike, sell a call at a higher strike. Profit if price > lower strike. Risk limited to net debit.

Call Credit Spread (Bearish): Sell a call at a lower strike, buy a call at a higher strike. Profit if price < short strike. Risk limited to strike width minus credit.

Put Debit Spread (Bearish): Buy a put at a higher strike, sell a put at a lower strike. Profit if price < higher strike. Risk limited to net debit.

Put Credit Spread (Bullish): Sell a put at a higher strike, buy a put at a lower strike. Profit if price > short strike. Risk limited to strike width minus credit.
