Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers about how StockHome works, subscription details, and how to contact us.

What is StockHome?

StockHome is a web-based tool that generates data-backed signals for option sellers and stock traders. It helps identify buy and sell points using indicators such as RSI, IV Rank, and Moving Averages.

How often are the signals updated?

Signals update automatically every hour using real-time data from APIs like Yahoo Finance and Finnhub. No manual refresh is required.

How does StockHome decide a Buy or Sell signal?

Our backend Python scripts analyze multiple technical indicators using threshold-based filters. A signal appears only when quantitative criteria are met.

Do I need any account or login to use it?

No login is required. All trading signals are publicly visible for research purposes, though premium data feeds may be added in future updates.

How can I contact StockHome support?

Use the Contact page linked in the header or footer. You can also email us directly at support@stockhome.me.
