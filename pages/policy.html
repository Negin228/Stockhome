Privacy Policy

Introduction

Stockhome (“we,” “us,” “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data. This policy applies to all users accessing our website and services.

Information We Collect

We collect personal data you provide directly, such as name, email, phone number, and any financial data required for account creation or trading activities. Automated data collection through cookies or analytics (e.g., IP address, browsing behavior) is also performed to improve user experience and security.​

Purpose of Data Collection

Personal information is used to create and manage user accounts, deliver our trading services, provide support, improve site features, and analyze trading habits. We may use your data for compliance purposes or updating you on policy or system changes.​

Sharing and Disclosure of Information

Purpose of Data Collection

We do not sell or rent your personal information. User data may be shared only with authorized third parties who assist us in delivering our services (e.g., payment processors, analytics providers), or as required by law or regulation.​

Cookies and Tracking

Cookies and similar technologies are used to authenticate users, store preferences, and analyze site usage. You may choose to disable cookies in your browser, but this may affect functionality.​

Data Security

Purpose of Data Collection

We implement measures such as encryption, firewalls, and secure access controls to safeguard your data. In the event of a data breach, affected users will be notified as required by law.​

User Rights

You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your data, and to withdraw consent where applicable. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details provided below.​

International Transfers

If personal data is transferred internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with relevant regulations.​

Contact Information
